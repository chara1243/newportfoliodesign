About Me

A motivated Environmental Science graduate with a strong academic background from Tribhuvan University. I specialize in environmental data collection and analysis, with hands-on experience in air quality monitoring, water quality assessment, and geographic information systems (GIS).

My research focuses on the intersection of household air pollution and public health, particularly investigating cooking practices and their impacts on indoor air quality. I'm passionate about translating scientific data into actionable policy recommendations for sustainable development.
